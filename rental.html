Hi there! If you're looking for a place to rent in Madison, we hope you'll take a look at our house. We think it's pretty great, and we think you will too.

My wife and I lived in the house for about five years before we needed to upsize due to a rapidly growing family. We bought a house in South Huntsville, but we couldn't bring ourselves to sell our first home. We're hoping to find a great couple, family, or really anyone to live in the house and enjoy it as much as we did.

The house is—in our possibly-biased opinion—super cute, and has a great layout, too. The living room, dining room, kitchen, and laundry are on the first floor along with a half-bath and some closet space. Head upstairs from the living room, and you'll find the gorgeous, renovated master bedroom and bathroom. There are also two more bedrooms which share a full bathroom.

Go back downstairs to the kitchen and you can head up a different set of stairs to the finished space over the garage. This space consists of a bonus room, bedroom, three-quarter bath, and a large closet, and is perfect for guests or children.

Outdoors, the house has a great yard in a great neighborhood. The front of the house has a porch and some gardens. The back yard is fenced in and has a storage shed, and plenty of space for kids, pets, entertaining, gardening, or whatever you like to do! The neighborhood is very friendly, safe, and there's a park within walking distance. And of course, you're in the heart of Madison, meaning the school system is one of the best in the state, and you're minutes from shops, restaurants, a YMCA, parks, and more churches than you can count.

Charming Neighborhood
The neighborhood is established and diverse, with families ranging from young couples to retired folks. There are sidewalks throughout, mature trees, and a park within walking distance.

Groceries, restaurants, and pharmacies are all within a mile. There's a Target three miles away. It's just five miles to Research Park and eight to Redstone Arsenal Gate 9. Downtown Huntsville is just thirteen miles away.
Wired for Ethernet

Renovated Master Suite
The master bathroom has a dual vanity with granite countertops, an all-glass shower, a jacuzzi tub and new hardwoods. The hardwoods extend into the master bedroom, and its two closets (one walk-in).
Extra Spaces
The space above the garage features a bonus room and an in-law suite which includes a bedroom, three-quarter bath and closet. The back yard has a large storage shed and a parking pad accessible from an additional driveway.
New Flooring
